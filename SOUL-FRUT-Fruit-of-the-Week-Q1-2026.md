## WEEK 1: MANGO 🥭
**Latin:** *Mangifera indica*
**Origin:** South Asia (India, Burma, Andaman Islands)
**Peak Season:** May - September
**Taste Profile:** Sweet, tropical, honeyed

### NUTRITIONAL PROFILE
- **Vitamin C:** 67% DV per cup (immune support, collagen synthesis)
- **Vitamin A:** 25% DV (vision, skin health, cellular regeneration)
- **Folate (B9):** 18% DV (DNA synthesis, cell division)
- **Fiber:** 3g per cup (digestive health, blood sugar regulation)
- **Antioxidants:** Mangiferin, quercetin, beta-carotene (anti-inflammatory, neuroprotective)

### HEALTH BENEFITS
1. **Immune System Fortification:** High vitamin C + polyphenols create synergistic immune response
2. **Skin Radiance:** Beta-carotene converts to retinol for cellular turnover
3. **Digestive Enzymes:** Amylases break down complex carbohydrates
4. **Eye Health:** Lutein and zeaxanthin protect against blue light damage
5. **Heart Health:** Fiber + potassium reduce blood pressure and cholesterol

### HOLISTIC PROPERTIES
- **Ayurvedic Classification:** Sweet, cooling, grounding (Kapha-reducing, Pitta-pacifying)
- **Traditional Chinese Medicine:** Tonifies Stomach Qi, clears heat, generates body fluids
- **Dosha Balance:** Reduces excess Pitta (inflammation, heat), increases Kapha in moderation
- **Energetic Temperature:** Cooling (ideal for summer, overheated conditions)

### METAPHYSICAL & SPIRITUAL PROPERTIES
- **Chakra:** Solar Plexus (Manipura) - personal power, confidence, transformation
- **Element:** Fire + Water (creative transformation, emotional flow)
- **Intention:** Manifestation, abundance, sweetness of life
- **Spiritual Symbolism:** Sacred in Hinduism and Buddhism (symbol of love, fertility, immortality)
- **Emotional Resonance:** Joy, playfulness, inner child healing
- **Meditation Focus:** Use mango to connect with tropical abundance consciousness

### WAYS TO CONSUME
**Fresh:**
- Diced in fruit salads
- Frozen chunks for smoothie bases
- Dehydrated as chewy snacks
- Grilled for savory applications

**Blended:**
- Mango Lassi (yogurt-based)
- Green smoothies (balances bitter greens)
- Protein shakes (tropical base)
- Sorbet bases

**Combinations:**
- **Synergistic Pairs:** Pineapple (digestive enzymes), coconut (healthy fats for vitamin A absorption)
- **Soul Frut Menu:** Tropical Sunrise Bowl, Mango Madness Smoothie, Island Vibes Blend

### RELATIVE & COMPLEMENTARY FRUITS
- **Family Relations:** Cashew, pistachio (same Anacardiaceae family)
- **Flavor Pairings:** Passion fruit, papaya, lime, ginger
- **Nutritional Complements:** Banana (potassium), spinach (iron + vitamin C absorption)
